What If I Tell You That Nothing Is Either Good or Bad?

By Ayzel

What if I tell you that nothing is either good or bad?

As humans, we love to label things. If a line of code breaks, we say it's bad. If our website works perfectly, we say it's good. But what if those things are just data points?

When you stop judging things as good or bad and just become curious, everything changes. A broken line of code isn't a failure—it's just a puzzle showing you how to learn something new.
